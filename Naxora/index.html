About RK Naxora

Two Decades of Spice Excellence from the Heart of India

Founded in 2001 in Rajkot, Gujarat, RK Naxora has grown into one of India's most trusted spice processing and export houses. We combine traditional knowledge with modern food-grade processing technology.

State-of-the-art Processing Plant 50,000 sq.ft. facility in Rajkot GIDC, processing 500+ MT per month with food-grade machinery
Farm-to-Pack Traceability Direct procurement from 500+ verified farmer partners across Gujarat, Rajasthan and Andhra Pradesh
In-house NABL Accredited Lab Every batch tested for moisture, colour value, microbial load, pesticide residues and aflatoxin
Global Export Network Active export relationships in USA, UK, UAE, EU, Japan, Australia and South-East Asia
