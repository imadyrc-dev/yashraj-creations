Our Story & Legacy

Who We Are

Born from Tradition, Built for Global Trade

RK NEXORA was founded in 2001 by Mr. Rameshbhai Patel, a third-generation spice trader with deep roots in Gujarat's vibrant spice culture. What began as a small procurement and wholesale business has evolved into a fully integrated spice manufacturing and export company.

Today, we operate a 50,000 sq.ft. food-grade processing facility, maintain direct partnerships with 500+ farmers across India, and serve clients in over 30 countries — from boutique importers to major food manufacturers.

Mission

Deliver pure, safe, and superior Indian spices to the world's tables — with integrity at every step.

Vision

Be the most trusted name in Indian spice exports, known for consistency, compliance, and care.

Our Facility

World-Class Processing Infrastructure

50,000 sq.ft. Plant

Dedicated zones for cleaning, drying, grinding, blending, and packing under one controlled roof in GIDC Rajkot.

Steam Sterilisation

Low-temperature steam sterilization preserves colour, aroma and active compounds while ensuring microbial safety.

NABL Accredited Lab

In-house testing for curcumin, ASTA colour, volatile oil, moisture, microbial counts, and pesticide residues.

Packaging Line

Automated form-fill-seal lines for 50–500g retail packs, 1–5 kg institutional bags, and 25–50 kg PP woven sacks.

Cold Storage

Climate-controlled warehouse ensuring raw material freshness and preventing moisture ingress during monsoon months.

Sustainable Operations

Solar-powered facility reducing carbon footprint. Zero-waste processing with by-product reuse for agricultural inputs.
