Our Story

About Us

Curio Couture began as a single cutting table in Jaipur, built on a simple belief — that occasion menswear should feel handed down, not mass produced. Every sherwani, bandhgala and kurta set that leaves our atelier carries the patience of a craft practised over generations, cut for a wardrobe that's meant to outlast the season it was bought for.

Handwork That Outlasts A Trend

Inside our Jaipur workshop, master karigars still hand-set every zardozi thread and cutdana bead, the same way it's been done for decades. We believe an occasion outfit deserves to be worn again and again — not once and forgotten — so every piece is fully lined, finished by hand, and built to hold its shape through years of weddings, festivals and celebrations still to come.
